About


The Great British Bake Off (GBBO) is a British television baking competitions 12 amateur bakers compete against each other over the course of twelve weeks. Each week, a theme is assigned (i.e. Pastry Week) and the contestants bake to impress the judges in the Signature, Technical, and Show Stopper rounds.

Show Popularity

Viewership for GBBO increases continuously until series 8, where it drops and plateaus thereafter. Why did the ratings drop almost 6 million? After series 7, the GBBO moved from BBC One to Channel 4, and previous judge Mary Berry and presenters Mel Giedroyc and Sue Perkins out of loyalty to BBC. Some viewers were also disappointed about the move since the show would now include advertisements.

Baker Demographics
