About


The Great British Bake Off (GBBO) is a British television baking competition where 12 amateur bakers compete against each other over the course of twelve weeks. Each week, a theme is assigned (i.e. Pastry Week) and the contestants bake to impress the judges in the Signature, Technical, and Show Stopper rounds.

Show Popularity

Viewership for GBBO increases continuously until series 8, where it drops and plateaus thereafter. Why did the viewership drop by almost 5 million in Series 8? After series 7, the GBBO moved from BBC One to Channel 4, and previous judge Mary Berry and presenters Mel Giedroyc and Sue Perkins left the show out of loyalty to BBC. Some viewers were also disappointed about the move since it meant the show would now include advertisements.

Baker Demographics

Unlike many television shows, GBBO's contestants make up all ages, ranging from as young as 17 to as old as 71. Females and males are equally represented on the show as well!
